Ongoing research project (since March 2025) on **"Development of Data Augmentation and
Sensor Fusion Technologies for Robust Autonomous Driving,"** funded by Hanwha Aerospace.

The project develops perception methods that stay reliable under challenging,
out-of-distribution driving conditions by combining multi-sensor fusion with
targeted data-augmentation strategies.
