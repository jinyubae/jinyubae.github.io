Professional Summary

I am an M.S. student in the Robotics Program at KAIST, working at the intersection of computer vision and robotics. My research builds models that perceive and anticipate human behavior — across egocentric vision, human motion and trajectory prediction, and 3D-aware multi-modal LLMs. My goal is to give robots and autonomous vehicles a human-aware understanding of dynamic environments so they can act safely and reliably alongside people.
